I'm with Information Interfaces Research Group at Georgia Tech, advised by Dr. John Stasko. My research spans information visualization, data science, human-computer interaction, and data journalism, focusing on enhancing the effectiveness, engagement, and trustworthiness of data analysis, communication, and consumption. I aim to design and develop techniques that empower data-driven content creators to produce insightful, engaging, and accurate data narratives efficiently, while also protecting their audiences from misinformation embedded within data artifacts.
